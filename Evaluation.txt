The evaluation function:

-If computer has 4 in a row horizontally, vertically, diagonally left or diagonally right then hundred points are added
-If human has 4 in a row horizontally, vertically, diagonally left or diagonally right then hundred points are reduced.
-If computer has 3 in a row horizontally, vertically, diagonally left or diagonally right and there is possibility of making 4 in a row then add fifty points.
-If human has 3 in a row horizontally, vertically, diagonally left or diagonally right and there is possibility of making 4 in a row then reduce fifty points.
-If computer has 2 in a row horizontally, vertically, diagonally left or diagonally right and there is possibility of making 4 in a row then add thirty points.
-If human has 2 in a row horizontally, vertically, diagonally left or diagonally right and there is possibility of making 4 in a row then subtract thirty points.

Using the above eval function the computer is able to consistently beat human player playing randomly.
